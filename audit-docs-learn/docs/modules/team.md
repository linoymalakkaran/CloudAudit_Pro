Team Management covers user onboarding and access control at the company/engagement level.

## Typical actions

- Invite team members
- Assign roles (admin/manager/senior/staff/client)
- Remove or deactivate users when needed

## Recommended controls

- Least privilege: users should only have access needed for their role
- Separate client portal access from internal audit team access
- Review access before report issuance
