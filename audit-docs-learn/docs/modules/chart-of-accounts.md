The Chart of Accounts (COA) defines the account structure used for trial balances, mapping, analytics, and financial statement presentation.

## What you do with the COA

- Import or configure the account list
- Maintain account codes, names, and types
- Support consistent mapping into reporting lines

## Recommended controls

- Restrict COA edits after the period starts (change control)
- Document any COA changes during fieldwork and their impact
